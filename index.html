Your Level Your level is calculated from your total earned XP — every quest you complete adds XP permanently. Early levels come quickly to reward momentum. Later levels demand sustained effort over months and years. The progress bar shows exactly how far through your current level you are. Your level never decreases — all progress is yours to keep.

1.00x
Your Title Your title reflects how the world would recognise someone with your current attribute levels. Everyone begins as The Beginner — not as an insult, but as an honest starting point. As your attributes grow through quests, your title will change to reflect the person you are becoming.
Your Rank Ranks follow the F → E → D → C → B → A → S progression. Your rank is determined by your level — F-Rank covers Levels 1 to 15, E-Rank covers 16 to 30, and so on upward. Beyond Level 100, ranks extend to S+, SS, SS+, and SSS — territory almost no one reaches. Each rank represents a genuine, observable shift in who you are.
Momentum Momentum reflects the compounding effect of consistent practice. Research shows that showing up regularly makes each session more effective — up to 50% more after two weeks of daily effort. Miss a day occasionally and it barely affects you. Fall away longer and momentum returns to baseline, ready to be rebuilt. Your progress is always yours to keep — momentum only affects how fast you earn it.

Strength Your physical capability — energy, fitness, and ability to exert yourself. A score of 10 is your baseline: functional and alive. This number grows without limit as you complete Strength quests — each point above 10 represents real effort invested in your physical self. There is no ceiling because human physical potential has no ceiling either. "Still figuring out where the gym is."

Intelligence Your capacity to learn, reason, and decide well. A score of 10 means you have a working mind and the ability to grow. Every Intelligence quest completed adds to this permanently — there is no upper limit because learning never stops. This is not an IQ score. It measures deliberate intellectual engagement over time. "Knows things. Working on knowing more things."

Growing this means you navigate disruption with increasing ease and find opportunity in chaos. Agility quests train you to break patterns and respond rather than react. "Flexible. Endurance Your ability to sustain effort over time — physically, mentally, emotionally. A score of 10 means you can get through a day. The rest are 'in progress'."

Charisma Your social presence — how you connect, influence, and build relationships. A score of 10 means you are capable of human connection. Growing this means people are drawn to you, trust you, and want to work with you. Charisma quests build your network and deepen genuine communication. "Likeable. People remember their name most of the time."
LUCK
10
Luck Luck cannot be raised directly — it is the average of all your other attributes. This is intentional. What people experience as luck is largely preparation, awareness, resilience, and strong relationships. Grow your core attributes and your luck rises with them. A high Luck score means you have become someone the world calls fortunate — because you earned it. "Lucky? Debatable. Getting luckier? Absolutely."
