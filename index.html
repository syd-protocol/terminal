[ ORDINARY PERSON ]

PLAYER

LEVEL 1
Your Title Your title reflects how the world would recognise someone with your current attribute levels. Everyone begins as an Ordinary Person — not as an insult, but as an honest starting point. As your attributes grow through quests, your title will change to reflect the person you are becoming.
Your Level Your level is calculated from the average of all five core attributes. It starts at 1 and rises as you consistently complete quests. A higher level reflects broad growth across all areas — not just one. Think of it as a measure of how much deliberate effort you have invested in yourself.
STRENGTH
10
Strength Your physical capability — energy, fitness, and ability to exert yourself. A score of 10 is your baseline: functional and alive. Growing this toward 100 reflects consistent physical effort and vitality. This is not a measurement of your body — it is a reflection of the effort you put into it.
INTELLIGENCE
10
Intelligence Your capacity to learn, reason, and decide well. A score of 10 means you have a working mind and the ability to grow. Completing Intelligence quests pushes this toward 100 — reflecting someone who learns deliberately and thinks carefully. This is not an IQ score. It measures intellectual engagement.
AGILITY
10
Agility How well you adapt to change and the unexpected. A score of 10 means you can function when life shifts. Growing toward 100 means you navigate disruption with ease and find opportunity in it. Agility quests train you to break patterns and respond rather than react.
ENDURANCE
10
Endurance Your ability to sustain effort over time — physically, mentally, emotionally. A score of 10 means you can get through a day. Growing toward 100 means you persist through difficulty without burning out. Sleep, consistency, and finishing what you start are the foundations here.
CHARISMA
10
Charisma Your social presence — how you connect, influence, and build relationships. A score of 10 means you are capable of human connection. Growing toward 100 means people are drawn to you, trust you, and want to work with you. Charisma quests build your network and deepen genuine communication.
LUCK
10
Luck Luck cannot be raised directly — it is calculated automatically as the average of all your other attributes. This is intentional. What people experience as luck is largely preparation, awareness, resilience, and strong relationships. Grow your core attributes and your luck rises with them.
